Expected Behavior by Screen Size:

  • Extra Large (1400px+): 4 columns, larger spacing, enhanced typography
  • Large Desktop (1200-1399px): 4 columns, optimized spacing
  • Medium Desktop (1024-1199px): 4 columns, compact layout
  • Small Desktop/Large Tablet (768-1023px): 2 columns, balanced layout
  • Small Tablet (640-767px): 2 columns, reduced spacing
  • Large Mobile (480-639px): 1 column, horizontal layout
  • Small Mobile (320-479px): 1 column, stacked layout
  • Extra Small (below 320px): 1 column, minimal spacing

Additional Features:

  • Landscape Mobile: 2 columns on landscape orientation
  • Touch Devices: Enhanced tap targets and feedback
  • High DPI: Optimized borders and effects
  • Accessibility: Improved focus states and contrast
  • Performance: Reduced animations on low-powered devices
